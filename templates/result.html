Understanding Your Results

Diabetic Retinopathy Stages:

  • No DR (0): No signs of diabetic retinopathy
  • Mild (1): Small areas of balloon-like swelling
  • Moderate (2): More blood vessels are blocked
  • Severe (3): Many more blood vessels are blocked
  • Proliferative (4): Most severe stage with new blood vessels

Next Steps:

  • Maintain good blood sugar control
