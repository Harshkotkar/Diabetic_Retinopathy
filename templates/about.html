About the Application

About This Application

This web application uses artificial intelligence to detect diabetic retinopathy from retinal images. It's designed to provide quick preliminary analysis and educational insights about retinal health.

How It Works

The application uses a deep learning model trained on thousands of retinal images to classify diabetic retinopathy into five stages. When you upload an image, the AI analyzes the patterns and features in the retina to determine the likelihood of diabetic retinopathy.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Flask (Python web framework)
  • AI Model: TensorFlow/Keras deep learning
  • Image Processing: PIL (Python Imaging Library)
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Understanding Diabetic Retinopathy

Diabetic retinopathy is a diabetes complication that affects the eyes. It's caused by damage to the blood vessels of the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye (retina).

Risk Factors

  • Duration of diabetes
  • Poor blood sugar control
  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Pregnancy
  • Smoking

Prevention

  • Regular eye examinations
  • Good blood sugar control
  • Blood pressure management
  • Healthy lifestyle choices
  • Early detection and treatment
